The Spa

Spa

Aesthetically simple and serene, the Spa is among
the largest in AGAZA,with over 2,787 square metres
of space to relax. The Spa offers 17 spacious treatment
rooms, two steam rooms, and a salon with hair.
Located on the ninth floor of the Hotel, the Spa adjoins
a sunlit indoor relaxation pool, co-ed whirlpool and
outdoor terrace overlooking nice view.

Spacious locker facilities feature steam rooms
and personal grooming amenities .A 24-hour,
325-square-metre (3,500-square-foot) fitness centre
is located on the eighth floor and is available
to hotel guests and residents only.
